The appellant was the tenant of a flat on the second floor of a building in Bombay. The tenancy was a monthly one, the rent being Rs. 215. The appellant wanted to go to the United Kingdom for treatment of his failing eye sight and he got into touch with the complainant through one Sayed for the purpose of making necessary ar rangements about the flat occupied by him in view of his intended departure.
